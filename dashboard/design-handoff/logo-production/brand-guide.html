Usage

Do

Preserve clear space

Minimum margin around the lockup equals the height of the "S" cap (~0.7× the icon).

Don't

Don't recolor ai or IQ

Signal Blue is non-negotiable on those characters. The blue is the brand.

Do

Use on solid grounds

Ink, Paper, or a quiet photograph. Maintain WCAG AA contrast.

Don't

Don't split the wordmark

SaijeIQ reads as one word. Never break it across lines or insert space.

Do

Outline before export

Convert text to paths in Figma/Illustrator for final asset delivery.

Don't

No effects

No gradients, glows, drop shadows, bevels, or outer strokes on the wordmark.
